Rules



Welcome players, to the Crypto Math Hunt! I am the Gamemaster and I will walk you through the clues

1. Each round will end within a set time limit specified by the game master.
2. Each team will have one leader.
3. Each team must figure out all the clues and solve all the problems.
4. The team that successfully solves the stage must approach the game master and explain the process of solving the stage, providing the final answer.
5. Only the leader of the team is allowed to communicate with the game master.
6. Each team is given only three chances to answer each round.
7. Points will be awarded based on the order in which teams provide correct answers. The first team to give the correct answer will receive the highest points.
8. Only the first seven teams to solve each stage will be awarded points.
9. Each stage is a separate puzzle, and stages do not correlate with each other.
10. After a set interval of time, hints will be released to help solve the stages.
11. No coordinator will communicate with you regarding the validity of your answers or the method of solving.
